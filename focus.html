Focus Areas

Thematic lenses through which DevWatch International examines governance, development, and strategic change in Africa and emerging economies.

Governance & Leadership

DevWatch examines how power, institutions, and leadership practices shape governance outcomes. We focus on accountability, state capacity, elite incentives, and the everyday politics of reform in African governance systems.

  • Public institutions and accountability
  • Leadership cultures and political incentives
  • Reform processes and implementation gaps
  • Federalism, decentralisation, and local governance

Development outcomes are shaped not only by resources, but by how financial systems are structured and governed. DevWatch analyses public finance, cooperative models, economic policy choices, and the political economy of growth and inequality.
